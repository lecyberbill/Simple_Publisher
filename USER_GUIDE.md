Welcome to **Simple Publisher**! This guide is designed to help you create stunning documents easily, even if you've never used publishing software before.

## üèó 1. Exploring the Interface

The screen is divided into four main areas:

1. **Title Bar (Top)**: This is where you manage your files (New, Open, Save) and where you can Undo or Redo your actions. You'll also find the export buttons (PNG/PDF) here.
2. **Canvas (Center)**: Your "paper." Everything you place here will be visible in your final document.
3. **Toolbar (Left)**: Your "toolbox." This is where you choose what to add: text, shapes, or drawing tools.
4. **Properties Panel (Right)**: When you click on an object (like a text box or a circle), its options appear here: change the color, font, size, and more.

---

## üé® 2. Creating Your First Object

### Adding a Shape
- Click on the **Square**, **Circle**, or **Star** icon in the left toolbar.
- The shape will appear on your page. You can move it by clicking and dragging, or resize it using the small white squares on the edges.

### Adding Text
- Click on the **"T"** (Text) icon.
- Double-click the text that appears on the page to type whatever you want.
- Use the right panel to change the font or make the text bold.

### Inserting an Image
- Click on the **Image** icon.
- Choose an image from your computer. It will be added to your project automatically.

---

## üñå 3. Drawing Like an Artist

Simple Publisher offers three ways to draw by hand:

- **Pencil Tool**: For thin, simple strokes.
- **Brush Tool**: For textured effects and patterns (dots, lines, diamonds).
- **Pen Tool (Bezier)**: For creating custom shapes point by point. Double-click a point to turn it into a curve!

### Smart Guides
When you move objects, magenta lines will appear to help you align them with other objects or the center of the page.
- **Magnet Button**: Toggle this feature on/off using the magnet icon in the toolbar.

---

## üì¶ 4. Managing Pages
Simple Publisher now supports multi-page documents!
- **Add Page**: Click the **"Add Page"** button at the bottom of the screen.
- **Switch Page**: Click on the thumbnail of the page you want to edit.
- **Delete Page**: Click the trash icon on a page thumbnail to remove it.

## üì¶ 5. Organizing Your Document

### Layers
Imagine your objects are stacked transparent sheets.
- Go to the **Layers** tab on the right.
- You can hide an object (eye icon) or lock it so it doesn't move by mistake (lock icon).

### Grouping Objects
If you've created a logo with several shapes and text:
- Select them all (or press `Ctrl + A`).
- Click the **Group** button at the top.
- Now they move together as one single block!

## üíæ 6. Saving and Sharing

- **Save**: Click the floppy disk icon at the top (or `Ctrl+S`).
- **Auto-Save**: Once you've saved your file for the first time, Simple Publisher will automatically save your changes every time you stop working for 2 seconds. Look for the "Enregistr√©" indicator at the top right!
- **Export as Image (PNG)**: To share your creation on social media or via email.
- **Export as PDF**: Ideal for high-quality printing.

## ‚å®Ô∏è Magic Shortcuts

To work faster, learn these few keys:
- **Ctrl + Z**: "Oops!" Undo your last action.
- **Ctrl + C / Ctrl + V**: Copy and Paste an object.
- **Ctrl + S**: Quick save.
- **Del** (or Backspace): Delete the selected object.
- **V Key**: Go back to the selection arrow.
